# Session 169

[[Freya]] spends some time in the [[Equinox Citadel]], seems like vibes are overall pretty good. 

Back all together at the [[The Solarian Respite]], we spend a while trying to create nicknames for each other. It doesn't go well. [[Adrienne Spout]] refuses "Addie", [[Freya]] volunteers "Weird Purple", for herself. We decide it might take some time. 

We get dressed up for our big night out, after a brief toy argument about which room [[Adrienne Spout]] is going to sleep in.

[[Samson]] talks a bit about both his transition from patrons and female partners. [[Rez]] is both curious and also hyping how great dating someone from [[Rez's Family]] must be. 

[[Eirian]] greets us and gives us the run down of the areas, stages, and bars (in order of [[Metallic Dragons]], according to her: Copper, Brass, Bronze, Gold, then Silver) at [[The Silver Tongue]]. She's going to go force [[Zmaj]] into joining us. [[E]] points out a [[Captain Mulgrace]], head of her guard [[Melnir]], who can help us if we need anything.
